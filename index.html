About me

Hey, my name is William Balaile and I’m a web artisan, which means I use web development tools to craft a one-of-a-kind web presence. All creations are made in collaboration with you to convey your brand across the web.

First is the research stage in which clients are consulted on the purpose, design and functionality of the website. Once this is complete, stage two begins where I create a prototype of the website to be reviewed by the client for changes to be made. After this we finalize the project and hand over to the client. A 12-month maintenance period begins where updates can be made.

I have had a fascination for building computers from a young age which led to eventually building one and learning how to program on it. When I’m away from my computer you can find me near football.
